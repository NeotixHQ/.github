# NeotixHQ

**Building the cognitive layer of work.**

NeotixHQ is an AI-native product studio exploring how people and organizations can turn fragmented information, conversations, workflows, and signals into structured context, better decisions, and action.

We are focused on a simple pattern:

> Fragmented signals → structured context → better decisions.

Most software helps people create, capture, and search more information. That is no longer the hard part.

The harder problem is preserving context, recognizing patterns, maintaining continuity, and helping people act with better judgment over time.

NeotixHQ exists to build systems for that missing layer.

---

## What we are building

NeotixHQ is currently developing early systems around memory, workflow intelligence, and controlled human-AI collaboration.

### Meridian by NeotixHQ

**Exploring temporal memory for modern work.**

Meridian explores how conversations can become structured memory, helping important patterns, decisions, and ideas resurface when they matter.

The core idea behind Meridian is that chat history is not memory. Useful memory requires structure, time, relevance, and the ability to surface what matters later.

Status: **In development**

---

### Augment-Labs by NeotixHQ

**Exploring AI-assisted hiring workflows.**

Augment-Labs explores how AI can help hiring managers turn job requirements, candidate signals, and interview preparation into a clearer decision workflow.

The goal is not to replace human judgment. The goal is to reduce friction, preserve context, and help hiring managers make better-prepared decisions.

Status: **MVP**

---

### Orin by NeotixHQ

**Exploring controlled personal AI collaboration.**

Orin explores how a personal AI layer can learn how to work with you over time while remaining transparent, bounded, auditable, and under your control.

Instead of changing itself automatically, Orin proposes improvements to its behavior, skills, and capabilities for your approval.

Status: **MVP**

## Principles

### Context over noise

We design systems that reduce fragmentation and make important signals easier to see.

### Workflows over demos

We build around real operational friction, not isolated AI tricks.

### Continuity over fragments

We believe the next layer of AI needs persistent context, not disposable interactions.

### Decisions over answers

The goal is not more output. The goal is better judgment and better action.
